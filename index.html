About.

Welcome to SkiMore, the ultimate destination for winter enthusiasts seeking unforgettable ski experiences in the breathtaking landscapes of Norway and Sweden. With a collective of five world-class ski resorts, we're dedicated to bringing you the thrill of the slopes, the beauty of the mountains, and the warmth of genuine Nordic hospitality.


At SkiMore, we're fueled by our passion for skiing and a deep appreciation for the pristine natural surroundings that Scandinavia offers. Our mission is to provide you with not just a ski vacation, but a journey into the heart of winter wonderland, where adventure meets relaxation, and memories are forged.


Our collection of five distinct ski resorts spread across Norway and Sweden brings together a diverse range of terrains and experiences. From the exhilarating slopes that challenge seasoned skiers to the gentle trails perfect for families and beginners, Skimore has something for everyone. Each resort offers its own unique charm, from traditional alpine chalets to modern amenities that cater to every comfort.


We believe that the true essence of any destination lies in its local culture, and Skimore is no exception. Immerse yourself in the warmth of Nordic hospitality, savor traditional cuisine that celebrates the flavors of the region, and connect with fellow ski enthusiasts who share your love for the slopes.


At SkiMore, we're committed to providing you with a seamless and unforgettable ski vacation. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring your safety, comfort, and enjoyment throughout your stay. Whether you're a seasoned skier seeking new challenges or a first-timer looking to learn, we're here to make your ski adventure exceptional.
